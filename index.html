About Sahid MI Engineering

Sahid MI Engineering stands as Ethiopia's leading manufacturer of heavy-duty vehicles, commercial trucks, fuel tankers, and underground fuel storage systems. For over 15 years, we have been delivering innovative, reliable, and high-performance solutions to meet the growing needs of industries across the nation.

Our expertise encompasses the design, engineering, and production of robust vehicles tailored to the demands of transportation, construction, and fuel logistics. With over 500+ vehicles delivered and hundreds of satisfied clients, our reputation is built on quality, precision, and durability.

At Sahid MI Engineering, we combine cutting-edge technology, premium materials, and rigorous quality control. Our team of skilled engineers ensures that each product not only meets but exceeds industry standards, delivering unmatched safety and reliability.

We are committed to empowering Ethiopia's industrial growth through customized solutions and innovative transport systems. From commercial trucks to specialized fuel tankers, our solutions are engineered to perform under the toughest conditions while maintaining efficiency and sustainability.

Discover the Sahid difference: innovation, expertise, and trust embedded in every vehicle we build.
